# CrediFlow AI

## Micro-Finance Credit Intelligence System

AI-powered alternative credit scoring  
for underserved communities.

---

### Problem Statement

Access to credit remains a major barrier  
for millions of informal workers and  
small businesses.

Traditional financial institutions rely on:

- Bank history
- Formal salary records
- Credit bureau scores

However:

Many small-scale traders, freelancers,  
and micro-entrepreneurs operate  
outside formal banking systems.

This results in:

❌ Loan rejections  
❌ Limited financial growth  
❌ Financial exclusion

---

### The Challenge

How can we evaluate creditworthiness  
for individuals **without formal credit history**?

Specifically:

Using alternative financial signals such as:

- Monthly income
- Spending patterns
- Savings behavior
- Transaction frequency

---

### Our Solution — CrediFlow AI

CrediFlow AI analyzes structured  
financial records from uploaded PDFs  
and generates an intelligent  
creditworthiness profile.

Powered by:

Google Gemini AI

---

### Solution Workflow

User uploads:

📄 Financial Statement PDF

System performs:

1. Extract financial data
2. Analyze using AI
3. Generate credit insights
4. Return decision-ready output

---
